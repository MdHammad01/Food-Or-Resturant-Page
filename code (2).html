Our Story

Established in the heart of Maulana Azad National Urdu University, Manuu Cafeteria has been serving students, faculty, and visitors with delicious and affordable meals since 2025.

We take pride in offering a diverse menu that caters to different tastes while maintaining high standards of hygiene and quality.
